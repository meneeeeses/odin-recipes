Hamburger

A sandwich consisting of a cooked meat patty on a bun or roll

Outside the Spanish-speaking area, it is more common to find the American name burger, short for hamburger.

Ingredients

  • Lean ground beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Butter
  • Sauces

Steps

  1. Grill the beef.
  2. Add the cheese.
  3. Top it off with topping.
  4. Add butter to the bottom of both buns.
  5. Bake the buns.
  6. Serve.
